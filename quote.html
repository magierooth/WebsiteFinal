My personal opinion about AI is that it depends more on how it is used and put into practice than whether it is good or bad. People are merely projecting their own technological misuse onto the device. AI can be quite helpful in some situations, such as when it comes to detecting cancer, aiding those who are ill, or even combating climate change. There are numerous obvious ways that AI is affecting, transforming, and reshaping our environment. I concur that we should be able to use AI as a tool for good and as a means of assistance. However, there is conflict with AI, and it poses a risk to human liberty, privacy, and some occupations. As an illustration, President XI doesn't permit other citizens to speak their opinions about the government based on what I've seen and read. I think people should express their opinions about what they think, whether they agree or disagree, without being penalized for doing so. Such things demonstrate the potential dangers of AI in the wrong hands. Even though I think AI can be helpful, I also think it has the potential to be dangerous. There is a middle ground that needs to be found, and my wish is that our future generations can do so and maintain the way things are for all time.
