The age of AI part 5 which is The Space Architect of Mars is a documentary that is hosted by Robert Downey Jr. covering the ways of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Neural Networks will change the world.

As the video is played I feel more engaged the more I watched it. The video is all about AI machines that will help build in space specifically in Mars. The AI Space Machine team had planned to build a house or so what we called a habitat that is strong enough to withstand the special calamity like sand storm in Mars as well as the ranging strong impact of asteroids. The material that is used in building the habitat is like a polymer that is strong enough to withstand the ranging impact or sand storm. The habitat is composed of a material that is similar to the components of mars. It has a structure that is most likely like an egg. It has a 3 layers that is stacked together most likely from dark to light to dirty to clean. The polymer structured habitat is most stable and can withstand the 90 ton weight of an excavator while the cement habitat cannot withstand it. So therefore, the polymer habitat is much stronger than the cement habitat. As for the field like in growing plants and harvesting plants, the AI is used.
